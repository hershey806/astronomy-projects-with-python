# Exploring the Theory Behind Sunspots and Utilizing Them to Determine the Rotation Period of the Sun

## Overview of Project

In this project, I explored one of the Sun's most recognizable features: sunspots. Sunspots are areas on the Sun's photosphere that are darker and cooler than their surroudings. Monitoring sunspots allows us to understand how and why solar flares occur.
